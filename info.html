🌾 Crop Tips & Best Practices

  • Rotate your crops every season to maintain soil fertility.
  • Use organic compost instead of chemical fertilizers for better yield.
  • Water early in the morning or late in the evening to avoid evaporation.
  • Choose high-yield and disease-resistant crop varieties.
