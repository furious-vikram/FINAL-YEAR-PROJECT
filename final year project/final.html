Storage Tips

Temperature Control

Different crops require different storage temperatures. Most fresh produce requires 0-4°C, while grains are best stored at 10-15°C.

Humidity Management

Maintain 85-95% humidity for leafy greens, 65-75% for dry crops like onions and garlic. Use moisture absorbers if needed.

Pest Prevention

Store crops in sealed containers and keep the area clean. Regular pest control treatments are recommended for long-term storage.

Packaging Guidelines

Use breathable packaging for fresh produce and airtight containers for dry goods. Proper labeling helps with inventory management.
